# Why Direct Bookings are the Future of Independent Hosting

In the last decade, Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) like Airbnb, Booking.com, and
MakeMyTrip have revolutionized how we travel. They provided hosts with a global
stage and guests with a trusted marketplace. But as they've grown, so have their
commissions — often reaching 15% to 20% per booking.

## The Shrinking Margin

For many hosts, these fees represent the difference between a thriving business
and barely breaking even. When you add up platform fees, cleaning costs, and
maintenance, the host is often left with only a fraction of the gross booking
value.

## Enter the "Direct Movement"

The "Direct Movement" isn't about quitting Airbnb. It's about owning your
relationship with your guests. Here’s why it matters:

1. **Brand Loyalty**: You are no longer just "a room on Airbnb." You are a
   brand.
2. **Repeat Bookings**: When a guest books direct once, they are 3x more likely
   to return.
3. **Control**: You set the rules, the cancellation policies, and the
   communication flow.
